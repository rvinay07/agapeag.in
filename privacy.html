Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

How Agape A.G Church handles information shared through this website.

Information We Collect

When you submit a prayer request, contact the church, join the media team, or send an email, we may receive your name, phone number, email address, and the message you choose to share.

How We Use Information

We use your information only for church communication, prayer support, pastoral care, ministry coordination, and responding to your request.

Prayer Requests

Prayer requests are treated with care and respect. Sensitive requests should be shared only with the details you are comfortable sending by email or WhatsApp.

Sharing Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share requests with pastors or trusted ministry workers only when needed for prayer, care, or follow-up.

External Links

This website links to YouTube, Instagram, Facebook, Google Maps, Gmail, and WhatsApp. Those services follow their own privacy policies.
