Gospel Ministry

Village Gospel outreach.

Testimonies

Brief Details

With a burden to reach the unreached, our church regularly carries the Gospel to surrounding villages. Through evangelism, prayer, house visits, Gospel meetings, and the distribution of Bibles and Christian literature, we share the love and hope of Jesus Christ.

By God's grace, many lives have been touched, families have been strengthened, and people have come to know the saving power of Christ. We remain committed to fulfilling the Great Commission by taking the Good News to every village and every heart.
